How do you prevent the spread of COVID-19?

Follow these steps to lower the risk of getting and spreading COVID-19. Read below:

Steps:

  • Step 1: Wash your hands often!

      Always wash your hands before eating food, before touching your face, after using the restroom, after sneezing or coughing, after touching your mask, and after touching animals
      These are of course the main times to wash your hands, but it is important to stay aware and wash your hands frequently to avoid getting the virus.
      Tips for sanitation:
        Use at least 60 % alcohol sanitizers
      Clean all household surfaces after bringing in supplies/goods from places with risk of covid(ex: grocery stores).

    Step 2: Maintain Distance!

      Always maintain 6 feet distance from people in public places.
      Especially when someone is sick, maintain distance. You never know if they might have the virus.
      Social Distancing is vital to preventing the spread of the virus.

    Step 3: Wear a mask!

      Always wear a mask, even though you maintain 6 feet distance from others.
      Wearing a mask prevents the virus from being breathed into your body. The mask filters out the dangerous particles.

    Step 4: Follow these additional tips for extra precaution!

      Tip #1: Go grocery shopping at odd times, when there aren't many people in the store.
      Tip #2: In office or work settings, always disinfect your surrounding before you sit. Use a disenfectant with at least 60 % alcohol
      Tip #3: Avoid social events and try to stay home as much as possible to avoid risk.
      Tip #4: Stay especially aware when going near infants, pregnant women, and the elderly. They have the highest risk.
